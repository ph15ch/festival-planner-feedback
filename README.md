# Festival Planner — Feedback & Support

This is the place to **report bugs** and **get help** with [Festival Planner](https://packshare.de).

---

## Before You Open an Issue

Search existing issues first — someone may have already reported the same thing or found a solution:

👉 [Search open issues](../../issues) · [Search closed issues](../../issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aclosed)

---

## Opening an Issue

Pick the template that fits best:

| I want to… | Use this template |
|---|---|
| Report something broken | [Bug Report](../../issues/new?template=bug_report.md) |
| Ask how something works | [Help Request](../../issues/new?template=help_request.md) |

Fill in as much detail as you can — screenshots and steps to reproduce make it much faster to help you.

---

## Feature Requests

Feature ideas are welcome! Open a blank issue and describe what you'd like to see and why it would be useful. No template needed.

---

## Discussions

For more open-ended questions, general feedback, or to chat with other users, head to the [Discussions](../../discussions) tab.

---

*Festival Planner is built and maintained by a solo developer. Response times may vary, but every report is read. Thank you for helping make the app better.*
